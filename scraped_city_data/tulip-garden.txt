

TULIP-GARDEN
A kaleidoscope of colours
Located at the base of the Zabarwan Mountain is the Indira Gandhi Memorial Tulip Garden Asia’s largest tulip garden and a key attraction in Srinagar. This seven-terraced garden is celebrated for its 48 varieties of tulip flowers, appealing to both adults and children alike.The garden is also home to a diverse array of other flowers, including daffodils, hyacinths, roses, narcissus, and various ornamental plants. A water channel flowing through the terraces enhances the garden’s beauty, providing an idyllic setting for memorable photographs.
